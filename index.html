UniGo is a comprehensive website designed specifically for university students, offering a range of convenient tools to enhance their daily lives. While currently in its initial stage, UniGo already provides two essential functionalities: "Free Room" and "Reservation Center". With plans to launch its first version in the 2023-2 semester, UniGo aims to expand its offerings to include more than five tools tailored to the needs of university students.

This website was entirely created by me, with the assistance of Bootstrap 5 and YouTube tutorials. It is responsive and could be helpful for others who wish to use this repository as a template for future front-end projects.
